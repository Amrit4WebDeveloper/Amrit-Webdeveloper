About Me

Welcome|
Hi, I’m Amrit — a 16-year-old web developer from India, currently studying in 1st PUC. I might still be in the early stages of my journey, but I’ve already fallen in love with coding and designing websites. What started as a small interest has quickly turned into a big part of my life — and I’m now offering my skills to real clients on platforms like Fiverr and through my personal website.

As a beginner, I know I have a lot to learn — and that’s exactly why I give my full attention to every project I take on. I’m not here to rush through jobs or deliver something halfway done. I care deeply about doing things the right way, even if it takes more time, because each project is an opportunity to improve, build trust, and make something I can be proud of.

I specialize in building simple, clean, and responsive websites — the kind that look good on any screen and are easy for your visitors to navigate. I pay attention to detail, I listen to your needs, and I’ll always communicate clearly with you throughout the process. You won’t get confusing jargon or missed messages — just honest work and a real desire to help your business grow online.

Joining platforms like Fiverr has helped me connect with people from different parts of the world, and every client I work with becomes part of my journey. I’m not just trying to build websites — I’m building experience, relationships, and a career I’m truly excited about.

If you’re looking for someone who’s enthusiastic, reliable, and genuinely eager to bring your ideas to life, I’d love the chance to work with you. Let’s create something meaningful — together.
